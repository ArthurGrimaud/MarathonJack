Rules : Marathon Jack
You begin the round with one card like the dealer. To win, you must have more points than the dealer by adding card in your hand. But take care, if you have more than 42 points you lose.
Click on stay when you don't want more cards
At the second round you can bet money. If you win the round, the dealer give you the double of your bet
Cards points :
2 to 9 the same value, Ace : 11 or 1 and face cards : 10
Take attention about the dealer and press "d" if you thinks it's the moment..
